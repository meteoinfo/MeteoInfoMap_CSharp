设置图形类型

图形类型可以用SetDrawType(string drawTypeString) 函数来设置，‘drawTypeString’可以设置为：

l         “contour”

l         “shaded”

l         “grid_fill”

l         “grid_point”

l         “vector”

l         “barb”

l         “streamline”

l         “station_point”

l         “weather_symbol”

l         “station_model”

l         “station_info”

l         “traj_line”

l         “traj_point”

l         “traj_startpoint”

l         “image”

l         “raster”

 

例如：

myApp. SetDrawType ("shaded")
